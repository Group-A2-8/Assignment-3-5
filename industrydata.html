Job Titles

Stephen’s ideal job is a Java Developer and the demand for this role in the IT labour market is the highest in comparison to the rest of his team members. The role of a Java Developer is to be involved throughout the whole development life cycle of a product. They must also be able to identify and analyse any issues or problems and be able to come up with efficient and effective solutions.

Alan’s is a Java Developer. Out of the roles in our group, Java developers rank the highest demand. Java developers build on core java programming, using mainly Oracle-based software to work with other systems and applications.

Akhil’s ideal job is a CTO (Chief Technology Officer), even though this does not have a huge no of opening. This role requires Technical and Management skills. It includes leadership quality and knowledge and deep understanding of IT technologies.

Jack’s ideal job is a Business Development Manager. The role is a combination of both business and technology and requires a strong understanding of both fields. Anyone in this role should be able to translate both business and IT concepts to those who specialise in the fields. A successful employee in this role will enhance the businesses' efficiency through the use of technology.
